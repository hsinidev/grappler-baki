Welcome to bakidou.org (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access or use the Site, services, and related features. By using the Site you consent to the practices described herein.

Content Data: your comments, reviews, manga preferences, and any uploaded content.

We process your information for the following legitimate purposes:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Account information is deleted upon request or inactivity for more than 24 months, except where retention is required for legal compliance.

We implement industry‑standard security controls, including:
